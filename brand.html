04 — Usage

Guidelines

Skedee's identity is built on clarity and confidence. These rules keep it that way across every surface.

✅  DO
Use the full lockup (icon + wordmark) wherever space allows
Use Skedee Gold as the primary CTA colour and key visual accent
Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the $ symbol
Match logo variant to background — dark logos on light surfaces, light logos on dark
Use Green exclusively for YES / positive outcomes, Red for NO / negative outcomes
Use the $ icon mark in tight spaces — app icons, favicons, social avatars
Keep all text on Skedee Gold in black (#000000) only
❌  DON'T
Don't place the logo on backgrounds that compromise contrast or legibility
Don't recolour, distort, rotate, stretch, or add effects to any logo asset
Don't use Skedee Gold as a large background colour behind body copy
Don't swap the typefaces — each font has a defined role and they aren't interchangeable
Don't use Green or Red decoratively outside of YES/NO market contexts
Don't abbreviate "Skedee" to "Skd" or any other shorthand in public copy
Don't recreate the logo in any other typeface — always use the provided file assets
