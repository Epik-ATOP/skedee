02
What Personal Data We Collect
Data You Provide to Us
Identity data: full legal name, date of birth, nationality
Contact data: phone number, email address, residential address
Verification data: government-issued ID, selfie/liveness images, Bank Verification Number (BVN)
Account data: username, password (hashed), transaction PIN (hashed), preferences
Financial data: bank account details (withdrawals only), deposit/withdrawal history, Wallet balance
Communications: messages to our support team, feedback, and other correspondence
Data Collected Automatically
Usage data: pages visited, features used, markets viewed and traded, session duration
Device data: IP address, device type, operating system, browser, device identifiers
Transaction data: full history of all trades, deposits, withdrawals, and positions
Log data: server logs including access times, errors, and security events
Data from Third Parties
Identity verification and KYC providers (BVN verification, document checks)
Payment processors who handle deposits and withdrawals
Fraud prevention and security services
Regulatory and law enforcement bodies where required by law

04
Who We Share Your Data With
We do not sell your personal data. We never share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

We may share your data with the following categories of recipients:

KYC/identity verification providers — to verify your identity and comply with AML obligations
Payment processors and financial institutions — to process deposits and withdrawals securely
Fraud prevention and security services — to detect and prevent fraudulent activity
Cloud hosting and IT infrastructure providers — who host and maintain the Platform on our behalf
Customer support tools — to manage your enquiries and complaints
Regulatory and law enforcement authorities — where required by Nigerian law, court order, or regulatory requirement
Professional advisors — lawyers and accountants, under strict confidentiality obligations
Business successors — in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of the business

All third-party providers are contractually required to handle your data in accordance with applicable data protection law.

07
Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data:

Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest
Passwords and PINs are hashed — we never store them in plain text
Multi-factor authentication (2FA) available for all accounts
Regular security assessments and penetration testing
Role-based access controls for our staff
Incident response procedures for detecting and responding to data breaches
In the event of a data breach likely to result in risk to your rights, we will notify the relevant authorities and affected Users in accordance with applicable law.

09
Children's Privacy

The Platform is strictly for persons aged 18 years and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from persons under 18. If we become aware that we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it immediately and close the associated account.

If you believe a minor has registered on the Platform, please report this to legal@skedee.ng immediately.

10
International Data Transfers

Skedee Inc. is based in Nigeria and primarily processes data within Nigeria. Some of our third-party service providers may be based outside Nigeria. Where we transfer your data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with the NDPA 2023, including contractual protections with our service providers.

11
Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email and/or a prominent notice on the Platform at least 7 days before taking effect. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this document always reflects the most recent version. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.

12
How to Complain

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us first at legal@skedee.ng and we will do our best to resolve it. If you remain unsatisfied, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) at ndpc.gov.ng.
