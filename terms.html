04
How Prediction Markets Work
Market Structure

Skedee operates a binary prediction market platform. Each Market presents a specific question with a binary YES or NO outcome. Each share is priced between ₦1 and ₦99, representing the market's implied probability that the outcome will occur. A share priced at ₦58 implies a 58% market probability of YES resolution. Upon resolution:

Shares on the correct side resolve at ₦100 per share;
Shares on the incorrect side resolve at ₦0 per share.
Market Resolution

Markets are resolved by Skedee Inc. based on official, publicly verifiable sources as specified in each Market's resolution criteria. Resolution is typically completed within 30 minutes to 24 hours of the relevant event concluding. Skedee Inc.'s resolution decision shall be final and binding.

Market Cancellation & Voiding

Skedee Inc. reserves the right to cancel, void, or suspend any Market if the underlying event is cancelled, the outcome cannot be determined, there is evidence of manipulation, or regulatory requirements necessitate it. In the event of a void, all stakes are returned to Users' Wallets in full.

Prediction markets are speculative instruments. Prices reflect collective market sentiment, not guaranteed outcomes. Past market accuracy is no guarantee of future performance.
