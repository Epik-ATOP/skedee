Market Rules
How it Works
Each day a new session opens at 2:00 PM WAT. You pick the bracket (₦5 range) where you believe the CBN official USD/NGN rate will fall at the close of business (10:00 PM WAT the following day). Only one bracket can win each session — the one whose range contains the final CBN close rate.

Resolution
The winning bracket is determined by the CBN official closing rate published by 10:00 PM WAT on the close day. All shares in the winning bracket resolve at ₦100. All other bracket shares resolve at ₦0. Resolution occurs within 1 hour of the official rate publication.

Edge Brackets
"Under ₦1340" wins if the rate closes below ₦1340.00. "Over ₦1380" wins if the rate closes above ₦1380.00 (inclusive). All other brackets are inclusive of both endpoints on the lower bound and exclusive on the upper bound (e.g., ₦1355–₦1360 covers ₦1355.00 to ₦1359.99).

Daily Cycle
Markets open every day at 2:00 PM WAT. The prior session's market closes and resolves the following day at 10:00 PM WAT. Both the opening and closing sessions may overlap briefly — you can trade both. Each session is a separate market with its own order book.

Data Source
CBN (Central Bank of Nigeria) official NAFEM window closing rate. If the CBN does not publish a rate on any given day, the market is voided and all positions refunded. Skedee reserves the right to use the most recent published rate if a delay occurs beyond 11:00 PM WAT.

Disputes
Contact support@skedee.ng within 6 hours of resolution. Skedee's decision is final.
