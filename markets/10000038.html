Market Rules
Resolution Condition
This market resolves YES for the team that wins 4 games in the 2026 NBA Finals series. The first team to win 4 games is crowned NBA Champion and all positions on that team resolve YES at ₦100 per share. Positions on the losing team resolve NO at ₦0.

Market Structure
Two separate outcome markets: "Knicks Win Championship" and "Spurs Win Championship." Each is priced so both markets sum to approximately ₦100, reflecting the exhaustive set of outcomes (one team must win). You can hold positions in both.

Data Source
Official NBA results. Resolution occurs within 2 hours of the final buzzer of the clinching game, once officially confirmed.

Series Suspension / Cancellation
If the Finals series is cancelled, indefinitely suspended, or abandoned before a champion is crowned, all positions are refunded in full. Skedee reserves the right to extend the market close date to accommodate schedule changes.

Disputes
Contact support@skedee.ng within 24 hours of resolution. Skedee's decision is final.
