Market Rules
Resolution Condition
This market resolves YES if WTI Crude Oil (NYMEX front-month contract) records an official closing price at or above $100.00 USD per barrel on any trading day before July 1, 2026 (23:59 UTC). If the target is not reached, the market resolves NO at market close on June 30, 2026.

Price Source
Official settlement price from the New York Mercantile Exchange (NYMEX) for the front-month WTI light sweet crude oil futures contract (CL1). Intraday highs do not count — only official daily closing/settlement prices are used.

Early Resolution
If WTI closes at or above $100 on any eligible day, the market resolves YES immediately after that session's settlement is confirmed (typically 6–8 PM WAT). Positions are settled within 1 hour of resolution.

Market Suspension
In the event of a trading halt or force majeure on NYMEX, Skedee reserves the right to pause this market until normal trading resumes. Extended halts may result in market cancellation and full refund of all positions.

Disputes
Contact support@skedee.ng within 24 hours of resolution. Skedee's decision is final.
