Market Rules & Resolution
What counts as a YES?
✅ "Artist Name" — solo release
✅ "Artist Name ft. Someone" — artist is the lead
❌ "Someone ft. Artist Name" — artist is the guest only
What type of release counts?
Any original track officially released on at least one major streaming platform (Spotify, Apple Music, Audiomack, Boomplay) before midnight June 30, 2026 WAT. Singles, EPs, and albums all count — as long as the artist is the lead act. Unofficial leaks, freestyles posted on social media only, and radio-exclusive plays do not count.
Resolution deadline
Midnight WAT on June 30, 2026. A release must be live and playable on streaming platforms — not just announced — by this time.
