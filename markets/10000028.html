Barcelona win the title if: (1) they win vs Celta; OR (2) they draw AND Real Madrid draw or lose vs Villarreal. Real Madrid win the title only if Barça draw/lose AND Madrid win.
✅ Case for YES (Barcelona)
They control their destiny — A win vs Celta seals it. Barcelona don't need any help from Real Madrid. Celta are mid-table with zero pressure; Barça are at home, motivated, and on form.
Flick's Barça are in form — 5 wins in their last 6 league games, 18 goals scored in that run. The attacking trident of Yamal, Lewandowski and Raphinha has been clinical.
Madrid face a motivated Villarreal — The Yellow Submarine need 3 points to secure 5th and Europa League football. They will not roll over for Madrid. Ancelotti's side has drawn 2 of their last 4 away games.
Historical home record — Barcelona have won 15 of 18 home league games this season. The Estadi Olímpic has been a fortress. A draw or loss at home would be a genuine shock.
Market consensus — At ₦71 YES, the market firmly backs Barça. Large YES positions have been opened consistently in the ₦68–₦73 range over the past week.
❌ Case for NO (Real Madrid)
Real Madrid always show up — Ancelotti's side have won 7 consecutive La Liga titles between 2012 and 2024 (non-consecutive). When it matters most, Madrid historically deliver. Their squad depth is elite.
Barça have nerves to manage — The pressure of a title decider at home, after leading all season, is immense. Barça bottled it on the final day in 2022/23. It has happened before.
Mbappé is in form — The French striker has scored in 4 consecutive La Liga appearances. If he fires vs Villarreal and Barça slip up, Madrid win it on goal difference — which is close.
The 29% price is value — A coin flip of 50/50 for whether Barça drop points vs Celta, crossed with ~60% for Madrid winning at Villarreal, puts realistic NO probability closer to 30–35%.

Market Rules & Resolution
Resolution Criteria
Resolves YES if FC Barcelona are confirmed as La Liga 2025/26 champions upon the conclusion of Jornada 38 on May 31, 2026. Resolves NO if any other club finishes top of the La Liga table.
Resolution Source
Official La Liga standings via laliga.com and confirmed by the La Liga official X account (@LaLiga). Resolution will occur within 2 hours of all Jornada 38 fixtures completing.
What if there is a points tie?
La Liga uses goal difference as the first tiebreaker, then goals scored, then head-to-head record. The official La Liga table at full-time determines the champion — Skedee will use the final official standings, not interim calculations.
What if matches are postponed?
If Jornada 38 fixtures are postponed beyond May 31 due to extraordinary circumstances, the market close date extends to the rescheduled date, up to June 14, 2026. Beyond that, Skedee will void the market and return all stakes.
