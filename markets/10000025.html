How it works
A daily multi-outcome market that resets every morning. Pick the hour with the heaviest rain — or bet on "No Rain". The slot with the most precipitation wins. Uses live Open-Meteo data — no manual resolution needed.

Lagos Rain Patterns
Lagos has a tropical savanna climate with two rainy seasons: the main season runs April–July, with a shorter season October–November. Average annual rainfall is ~1,600mm. During peak season, rain can fall on 15–20 days per month.

Strong YES signals: cloudy humid morning, temperature 28–32°C, humidity above 85%, afternoon thunderstorm forecast. Strong NO signals: clear sunny morning, low humidity, easterly winds off the Sahel.
Market Rules & Resolution
Resolution Criteria
Resolves to the hourly slot with the highest precipitation at Lagos (6.5244°N, 3.3792°E) between 8:00 AM and 7:00 PM West Africa Time (WAT, UTC+1). Ties go to the earliest of the tied slots. Resolves "No Rain" if the entire window records zero precipitation. The winning outcome pays ₦100 per share; all other outcomes pay ₦0.
Data Source
Open-Meteo real-time weather API using WMO-standard observations for the Lagos metropolitan area. The winning hour is determined by the maximum value in the hourly.precipitation field for slots 8AM–7PM WAT.
Daily Reset
A completely fresh market opens at 8:00 AM WAT every calendar day. Previous day results do not carry over. The page state (LIVE / SETTLED / PRE-MARKET) is determined automatically by the current time.
Disputes
Contact support@skedee.ng within 24 hours of resolution with Open-Meteo API evidence. Skedee's decision is final in cases of data source conflict.
